ReadOutLoud Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-03

ReadOutLoud is a macOS app that converts English text into spoken audio files. This page explains what the app does with user content and what contact options are available.

1. Text sent for speech generation

To generate audio, ReadOutLoud sends the text entered or imported by the user to the Microsoft Edge TTS online voice service. Without this network request, audio generation will not work.

2. Local files

If a user imports a local .txt file, the file is read locally by the app. If a user chooses an output folder, generated .mp3 files are saved locally to that selected location.

3. Personal data

ReadOutLoud does not require account registration, does not include advertising, and does not intentionally collect name, phone number, email address, or similar personal identifiers inside the app itself.

4. Support contact

If you contact support by email, your message will be processed by your email provider and the recipient's email provider as part of normal email delivery.
