## Full Description Draft

ReadOutLoud is a lightweight macOS app for generating English reading audio quickly.

It is designed for:

- English teachers who need preview, dictation, or follow-along audio
- Students who want to create their own listening and shadowing materials
- Parents and tutors who need simple, repeatable pronunciation audio

Key features:

- Paste or import text and generate audio in batches
- Multiple English voices, including US and UK options
- Adjustable speed
- Generate separate files or merge everything into one track
- Built-in triple-play mode for repeated listening and shadowing

Important note:

ReadOutLoud requires an internet connection because it uses the Microsoft Edge TTS online voice service to generate audio.
